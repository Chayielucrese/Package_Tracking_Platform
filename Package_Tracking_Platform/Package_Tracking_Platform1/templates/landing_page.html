Why Use Our App?

Our package tracking platform offers a seamless and reliable way for you to keep track of your shipments. With real-time updates and detailed tracking history, you can always know where your package is and when it will arrive. Our user-friendly interface and comprehensive tracking features ensure you have peace of mind with every shipment.

How It Works

  • A client ships one or more packages and a package is shipped by only one client. The shipping could simply be the client ordering something online and asking to have it delivered to an address.
  • We keep track of the packageâ€™s current location. When the order is placed, the package is initially at the store where it is ordered from.
  • The package is then moved from one warehouse to another until it gets to the destination.
  • Anytime the package arrives at a warehouse, an employee logs (using the application) that the package has arrived at the warehouse.
  • We keep track of which employee scanned which package.
  • We also keep track of the different warehouses a package has passed through.
  • When a client places an order, they are given a tracking ID which they use to track their packages in real-time.
  • When they open the client portal of the app, they see a map and a search box where they paste their tracking ID.
  • Once the tracking ID is pasted, the map shows them the current location of their package, when it arrived there, and also the different other warehouses it has been to.

Help & FAQs

How do I track my package?

Simply enter your tracking ID in the search box on the client portal to see the current location and tracking history of your package.

What should I do if my package is delayed?

If your package is delayed, please contact our customer service team with your tracking ID for assistance.

Can I track multiple packages at once?

Yes, you can track multiple packages by entering their tracking IDs one by one in the search box on the client portal.
